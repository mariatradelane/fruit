Company Overview

Fruit World is the website of Maria Tradelane, specialized in sourcing fresh fruits and vegetables from New Zealand, Australia, Chile, Italy, Netherland, South Africa, and Egypt.

Our  company is located in the southern part of India, which is established in the year2017.  We have obtained a good experience of fruits and vegetables after working fifteen years in the fresh fruitindustry. 

We offer comprehensive sourcing of fresh fruits for our clients.Our vertical Procurement solution enables us to procure most of the fresh fruitsdirectly from growers,  we also have extensive contact with exporters and packers, which will enable our clients toavail themselves the most competitive rate. 

We mainly source Royal Gala, Granny, Red Apple, Grapes, Oranges, Mango Kent, Mango R2E2, Rock Melon, Pomegranate and Cherry.

We source mainly for Indian and Middle East importers. We are open with flexible and reasonable terms for everyone who is willing to partner with us.
